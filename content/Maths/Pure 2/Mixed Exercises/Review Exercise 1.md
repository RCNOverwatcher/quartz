# Review Exercise 1

> [!Example]- Exercise 11
> Solve the inequality: $|4x+3|>7-2x$
> 
> - $|4x+3|=7-2x$
> - $4x+3=7-2x$
> - $6x=4$
> - $x=\frac{4}{6}=\frac{2}{3}$
> ---
> - $-(4x+3)=7-2x$
> - $-4x-3=7-2x$
> - $-2x=10$
> - $x=-5$
> ---
> $x<-5$ or $x> \frac{2}{3}$
