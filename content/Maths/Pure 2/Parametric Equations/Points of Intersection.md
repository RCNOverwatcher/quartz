# Points of Intersection 

> [!Example] Example Question 1
> - $x=4at^2$
> - $y=a(2t-1)$
> - Passes through (4,0)
> ---
> - $a(2t-1)=0$
> - $2ta-a=0$
> - $2ta=a$
> - $2t=1$
> - $t=\frac{1}{2}$
> ---
> - $4\times a\times\frac{1}{2}^2$
> - $4\times\frac{1}{4}\times a=4$
> - $a=4$

> [!Example] Find where $x=t^2$, $y=2t$ intercepts $x^2+y^2-9x+4=0$
> - $t^4+4t^2-9t^2+4=0$
> - $t^4-5t^2+4=0$
> - $t=^+_{-}2, ^+_{-}1$
> - Co-ordinates are $(4, 4), (4, -4), (1, 2), (1,-2)$
