# Working with Natural Logarithms

> [!Info] Natural Logs
> - Natural logs are essentially a log with base $e$. E.G. $\ln x=\log_{e}x$
> - $e^{\ln x}=\ln(e^x)=x$

> [!Example] Example of equations with natural logs
> - $e^{2x+3}=7$
> - $2x+3=\ln7$
> - $2x=\ln(7)-3$
> - $x=\frac{\ln(7)-3}{2}$
