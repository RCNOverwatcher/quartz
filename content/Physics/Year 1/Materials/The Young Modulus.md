# The Young Modulus

> [!Tip] Young Modulus 
> $$\text{young modulus} = \frac{\text{stress}}{\text{strain}}$$


> [!Info] Deriving the Young modulus 
> $$\text{stress} =\sigma=\frac{F}{A}$$
> $$strain=\varepsilon=\frac{\Delta L}{L}$$
> $$E=\frac{Fl}{A \Delta L}$$
