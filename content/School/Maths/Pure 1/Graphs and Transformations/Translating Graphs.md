# Translating Graphs

> [!Info] Transforming Graphs
> - Start with $f(x)=$ the equation you want.
> - Work backwards to find the equation you want.
> - E.G $y=\frac{2}{x+1}$
> - $f(x)=\frac{1}{x}$
> - $f(x+1)=\frac{1}{x+1}$
> - $2f(x+1)=\frac{2}{x+1}$
> - $f\left(\frac{ x+1}{2} \right)$
