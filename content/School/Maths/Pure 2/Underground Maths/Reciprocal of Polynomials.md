# Reciprocal of Polynomials

> [!Info] Express $x^3-3x-2$ as the product of three linear factors.
> - Set $x=2$
> - $8-6-2=0$
> - Therefore $x-2$ is a factor 
> ---
> - Set $x=-1$
> - $-1+3-2=0$
> - Therefore $x+1$ is a factor 
> ---
> - The factored cubic is $(x-2)(x+1)(x+1)$
